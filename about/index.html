About this website

This website was built to collate the data on the Renewable Heat Incentive (RHI), and present it in a more readable format.

  • What is the Renewable Heat Incentive?

    The Renewable Heat Incentive is a payment system for the generation of heat from renewable energy sources

  • What does this website show?

    This website shows the payments received by companies under the RHI scheme.

    Currently it is limited to payments made under the non-domestic RHI scheme, but it will be updated once the details of payments made under the domestic RHI scheme have been released.

  • Where is the data from?

    This data is a collection of the data recently released by the Department for the Economy.

    Company data was cross-referenced with public company records supplied by OpenCorporates.com.

  • When was the data last updated?

    The data shows payments made up to the 28th February 2017.
